Lactation QI - Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Lactation QI for mobile devices that was created by SDRC (Siddha Development Research and Consultancy (P) Ltd).

Lactation QI provides downloading and data collection for various forms, review, edit and send the filled forms to server and manage (add and update) the forms.
Information We Collect
Lactation QI receives or collects information when we operate and provide our services, including when you install, access or use our services.
Information You Provide
  • Your Account Information : Your email is being captured.
  • Customer Support : You may provide us with information related to your use of our services, including the submitted forms. For example, you may send us an email with information relating to our app performance or other issues.
Automatically Collected Information
  • Your Connections : To synchronize forms with the server and to fetch latest data we use network connection.
  • Your Device State : To keep track of devices through which the forms are getting submitted, we use UUID number of your device.
  • Your Device Storage : To store synced and saved forms we use your device's internal memory..
